## Energy & Delivery Tips

### Voice & Pace
- **Vary your pace:** Slow for emphasis, fast for excitement
- **Use pauses:** After big numbers or key points
- **Volume changes:** Quieter for "imagine..." louder for achievements

### Body Language
- **Open stance:** No crossed arms
- **Hand gestures:** Emphasize numbers with fingers
- **Eye contact:** Scan the room, don't stare at slides
- **Move:** Don't stand frozen, walk during transitions

### Confidence Builders
- **Practice out loud:** 10 times minimum
- **Record yourself:** Check for filler words
- **Time precisely:** 2:45 target (leave buffer)
- **Have water:** Small sips if needed

### Emergency Recoveries

**If you forget something:**
"There's so much to cover, but the key point is..."

**If technical issues:**
"While that loads, let me tell you about..."

**If tough question:**
"Great question. The short answer is... Let's discuss details after."

**If running over time:**
Skip slide 7 (competition) - least critical
